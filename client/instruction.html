Getting Started

Follow these steps to search behavioral questions, view sample answers, and manage your own submissions (attempts).

1

Sign Up or Sign In

  1. Go to Sign Up to create an account, or Sign In if you already have one.
  2. Not using auth yet? You can still browse questions and answers. Saving your attempts to Submissions may require sign-in later.
2

Browse & Search Questions

  1. Go to the Questions page (homepage of the app).
  2. Use the search bar to filter by Keywords and Tags. For example, try keywords leadership and tags conflict, communication.
  3. Click Search to fetch matching questions. Use Prev/Next to navigate pages.
3

Open Question Detail & Read Sample Answers

  1. Click a question title to open its detail page.
  2. Use Show answers to show suggestion answers (e.g., student, experience, pm, sales).
  3. Read the body of the question and refer to sample answers for structure and phrasing ideas.
4

Practice

  1. Go to the Practice page. There is a random question.
  2. Draft your answer.
  3. Click Compare my answer to compare your answer with the sample answer or Clear to clear your answer.
5

View All Your Submissions

  1. Open Submissions to see a paged list of your attempts.
  2. Click an item to view the full details.
